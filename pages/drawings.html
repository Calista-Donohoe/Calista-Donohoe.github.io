about

I've been drawing since I was very young. One of my favorite ways to document the different 'chapters' of my life is by keeping a sketchbook; they function like visual journals of my homes, thoughts, amusements, preoccupations, and adorations. On this page, you'll find a gallery of sketches from 2014 to present day.

supplies

I've experimented with many different materials over the years. These are some items that I highly recommend:

  1. Sakura Pigma Micron pens are great for creating outlines and adding small details
  2. Staedtler Mars Lumograph pencils are excellent for sketching
  3. A Copic marker set makes a huge difference for shading larger pieces; the warm greys are my favorite
